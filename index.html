GAPS: Tangible Landscape Activities

Grade level/course: Grades 9-12

Description

These activities lead students through an introduction to geospatial computation simulation and modeling using a geosptial tangible interface-Tangible Landscape.

Specifically, these activities outline various topics of geohazards, geospatial concepts, modeling, and technologies.
